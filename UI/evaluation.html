How productive has been?

Output

How much delivered (not necessarily to customers), valuable, finished work did they get done?

Working a lot of hours is generally not related to productivity and, after a certain point, indicates inefficiency. It is more valuable if they are able to maintain a sensible work/life balance and use their time in the office efficiently, rather than working around the clock.

Group

How much do they contribute to company process, hiring, integrating people into the team, improving workflow, amplifying their colleagues, or writing tools used by others? Generally, being a group contributor means that they are making a tradeoff versus an individual contribution.

Contributions to culture, and the wellbeing of others would be included in this category.
